ATTACK TYPES

Projectile
- Instantly hits the targeted panel
- Blocked by Obstacles directly in front of target
 - Includes Obstacles in front of that Obstacle as well
- Lose 1 Accuracy Tier if misaligned

Melee
- Must step onto panel in front of target
 - Some chips shift this standing placement (Kunai, Lance, etc)
- Gain 1 Accuracy Tier if already positioned

Aerial
- Strikes targeted panel from above
- Must be able to impact panel
- Flies over Obstacles
- Gain 1 Accuracy Tier if precisely 3 tiles away

Floor
- Affects every panel on its path
- Can fall through holes or stop on Obstacles

Obstacle
- Replaces object on panel in front of user

Personal
- Exists on same panel as user

Instant
- Immediately materializes on the target


ATTACK EFFECTS

Add
- Copies the damage taken to the last chip selected this turn

All
- Chip damage affects all selected chips

Rand
- Copies the damage taken to a randomly selected chip this turn

Delete
- Instantly destroys the targeted chip

Smash
- Instantly destroys both Slot-In chips

Stun
- Prevents movement and end-of-turn attack

Stop
- Prevents next chip from being used and gauge from charging for duration of turn

Mind
- Randomizes movement and prevents usage of gauge for duration of turn

Snatch
- Steals the primed chip from the program deck for duration of battle

Lucky
- The targeted chip will replace the Zenny reward at the end of the battle

Pierce
- Damage Obstacles and Guards while still damaging target

Break
- Destroys Obstacles and Guards en route to target

Shove
- Pushes the entity by specified amount (default: infinite)
- Obstacles that collide with other entities are broken and deal damage equal to remaining health
- Also shifts affected Sand Panels

Pull
- Pulls the entity by specified amount (default: infinite)
- Obstacles that collide ith other entities are broken and deal damage equal to remaining health
- Also shifts affected Sand Panels

Skip
- Ignores any Guards and Status attached to the target

Require
- Only the specified panels or objects can spawn the attack

Drain
- Lowers the target's gauge by specified amount

Defense
- Takes damage for user

Counter
- Delivers a counterattack when condition is met

Interrupt
- Cancels the incoming attack type from occurring

Freeze
- Turns Sea Panels to Ice Panels and Heat Panels to Basic Panels

Slip
- Applies a -1 Accuracy penalty per stack

Steal
- Turns the affected territory into the user's

Panel
- Changes the affected panels to the type indicated

Crack
- Turns traversible non-Metal panels into Cracked, and Cracked panels into Pits

Swirl
- Turns Sand or Sea Panels into Basic and powers up the attack
 - Sand grants a Damage Bonus
 - Sea turns the attack to [Aqua]

Double
- Launches two attacks if it connects

Triple
- Launches three attacks if it connects

Quad
- Launches four attacks if it connects

Quint
- Launches five attacks if it connects

Dice
- Launches between 1 to 6 attacks if it connects

Meteo
- Launches the specified amount of attacks, each rolled for dodge individually

Heal
- Heals target instead of damaging

Absorb
- Damages target and heals user

Bleed
- Deals damage equal to health lost

Delay
- Executes attack after end-of-turn actions

Copy
- Repeats damage that the user dealt

Power
- Increases the specified type's damage by the specified amount


VIRUS TRAITS

Armor
- Reduces damage from each hit by specified amount
- Turns off after first attack per turn

Charge
- Gains the specific gauge charging bonus

Sighted
- Gains the specified accuracy for aligning with target

Trail
- Changes the panel to the specified type in its wake

Bolted
- Cannot be moved around by attacks

AirShoes
- Can safely occupy all panels

Suspended
- Has AirShoes until struck by the specified damage type

Unflinching
- Cannot be stopped nor interrupted

Unstoppable
- Cannot be stunned nor confused

Sunburn
- The target takes the specified damage per action while exposed to direct light

Deficiency
- The target takes the specified AllAdd damage per turn while shrouded in darkness
